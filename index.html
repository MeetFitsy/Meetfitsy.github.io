Meet Fitsy!

Fitsy is your AI fitness coach which can provide you with training sessions for Swimming, Cycling, Running, Triathlon and also help answer any FAQ's you have about fitness.

Fitsy will also be able to help you with general fitness too such as HIIT Sessions or gym workouts and is always learning new things. The more you interact the smarter Fitsy will become.
